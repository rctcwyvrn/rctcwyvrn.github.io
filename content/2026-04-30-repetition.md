_Repetition legitimizes._

Any [Adam Neely](https://youtu.be/LlmTWlaWs_o) fans in my readerbase?

_Repeition legitimizes._

That's kinda why I write the way that I do.

In order to convey an idea properly, [in order to teach something through writing](/woolf-quote), you need to repeat yourself.

And in repeating yourself, you legitmize the idea that you're trying to convey.

_Repeition legitimizes._

It's a simple lesson and a simple phrase, but it's very powerful.

You can do so much with this idea.

Become a better writer. Repeating yourself in your writing to better convey an idea

Become a better musician. Repeating your ideas to legitimize them as Adam describes

Become a better speaker. Repeating yourself in your speaking to drive home an idea

Become a better teacher. Repeaeting yourself in different ways to teach anyone _anything._

Just remember, repetition of an idea legitimizes it, conveys it, teaches it, turns it from just some words into an _idea_.

_Repetition legitimizes._
